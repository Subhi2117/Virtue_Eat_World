Why choose a Virtue Eat World franchise?

📖

Proven Recipes

Every chaat and puri recipe is standardised and tested across 12 outlets, so quality never depends on guesswork.

🎓

Hands-On Training

A two-week onboarding at our flagship outlet covers prep, hygiene protocol, and live-counter service.

🚚

Supply Chain Support

Centralised sourcing for puris, chutneys, and tea concentrate means consistent taste with lower prep effort.

📣

Marketing Kit Included

Launch posters, social templates, and a local-area promo plan for your opening month.

💰

Low Break-Even Period

Most partners recover their setup cost within 8–14 months given the low per-plate cost of street food.

🤝

Ongoing Field Support

A regional manager visits monthly to audit hygiene, taste, and service standards at your outlet.
